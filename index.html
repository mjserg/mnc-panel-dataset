📘 About the Dataset

The MNC-Panel Dataset is an academic resource for analyzing multinational corporations (MNCs) in the context of their institutional, economic, political, and cultural environments. It merges:

  • Firm-level data on financials, governance, employment, and internationalization
  • Country-level data on institutions, development, democracy, culture, and sustainability

This dataset is built for scholars in international business, strategic management, political economy, and comparative institutional research.

Coverage: 560 manufacturing MNCs · 85 countries · 15 years (2010–2024)

🌍 Why It Matters

  • Supports publications in top journals (e.g., JIBS, SMJ, WPE)
  • Structured for multilevel models and panel-based empirical analysis
  • Used in graduate and doctoral programs in Sweden and internationally

📊 Use Cases & Analytical Possibilities

The dataset supports a wide range of academic inquiries, including:

  • How democracy and institutional quality shape corporate performance
  • Effects of cultural distance on internationalization strategies
  • Political instability and board structure adaptation

Recommended methods:

  • Fixed-effects / Random-effects modeling
  • Multilevel modeling (HLM)
  • Difference-in-Differences (DiD)
  • Interaction term designs (e.g., firm × country)
