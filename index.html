The MNC-Panel Dataset is an academic resource for analyzing multinational corporations (MNCs) in the context of their broader institutional and national environments. It merges:

  • Firm-level data on financials, governance, employment, and internationalization
  • Country-level data on institutions, development, democracy, culture, sustainability, innovation, and policy environments

This dataset is designed for scholars in international business, strategic management, political economy, and comparative institutional research.

ğŸ“Š Use Cases & Analytical Possibilities

The dataset supports a wide range of academic inquiries, including:

  • How democratic quality and institutional stability affect firm performance and governance
  • How cultural distance shapes foreign entry modes, ownership, and international strategy
  • How political risk and civic freedom influence board composition and decision-making
  • How economic development and sustainability factors affect multinational location choices

Recommended methods:

  • Fixed-effects / Random-effects modeling
  • Multilevel modeling (HLM)
  • Difference-in-Differences (DiD)
  • Interaction term designs (e.g., firm Ã— country)
